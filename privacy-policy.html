Privacy Policy

1.  What Info We Grab
    *   Info You Give Us:
        *   Like your name, email, and how to reach you.
        *   Stuff for your account, like your username and password.
        *   How you pay, like billing info.
        *   What you say to us in messages.
        *   Anything else you feel like sharing.
    *   Info We Get Automatically:
        *   Your IP address, what kind of browser you use, what device you're on, and your operating system.
        *   How you use our stuff – pages you check out, what you click, how long you hang around.
        *   Where you are (if you let us track that).
        *   Cookies and similar tech that track stuff.
    *   Info from Other Companies:
        *   We might get info from partners, analytics peeps, or social media if you use them to get to us.
2.  How We Use Your Info
    *   To give you our Services and keep them running.
    *   To process your orders and payments.
    *   To chat with you, like with customer support.
    *   To send you updates, deals, and promos (if you say it’s cool).
    *   To make our site, products, and service better.
    *   To spot and fix tech or security problems.
    *   To follow the law.
3.  Sharing Your Info
    *   We don’t sell your personal info. We might share it, but only if:
        *   We need to with service people who help us do business (like payment companies or hosting).
        *   With our own family of companies for behind-the-scenes stuff.
        *   If the law says so, like if we get a legal order.
        *   If we sell the business or merge with someone else.
        *   If you say it’s okay.
    *   Everyone we share with has to keep your info safe and only use it how we say.
4.  Cookies and Tracking
    *   We use cookies to:
        *   Remember what you like and how you set things up.
        *   Make our website better and understand how people use it.
        *   Show you stuff and ads you might like.
    *   You can control cookies in your browser, but turning them off might break some things.
5.  How We Keep Your Info Safe
    *   We have tech and rules to protect your info from getting hacked, changed, seen, or wrecked. But no internet thing is 100% safe, so we can’t promise it’s bulletproof.
6.  How Long We Keep Your Info
    *   We only keep your data as long as we need it to give you our service, follow the law, or sort out issues. After that, we delete it or make it anonymous.
7.  Your Rights
    *   Depending on where you live, you might have the right to:
        *   See your data and get a copy.
        *   Fix or delete your data.
        *   Stop us from using your data in certain ways.
        *   Take back your permission.
        *   Complain to the people in charge of data protection.
8.  If You're Not 
    *   If you're using our service from somewhere else, your info might get sent to and handled in countries with different data laws. We'll make sure those transfers follow the rules and that your info is still safe.
9.  Kids' Privacy
    *   Our stuff isn't for kids under 13 (or whatever the minimum age is in your country). We don't mean to get data from kids. If you think a kid gave us their info, tell us so we can remove it.
10. Changes to This Policy
    *   We might change this policy sometimes. We'll post the new version here with a new Last Updated date. If we make big changes, we'll let you know directly.
